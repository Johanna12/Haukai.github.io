Welcome to The Haukai Restaurant


Welcome to our restaurant! Whether you are a local, a tourist, a business traveller, we know that you need to find a good restaurant to meet and greet clients. Why not pop in to our restaurant in Kerikeri and taste our unique cuisine of Maori-style foods mixed with New Zealand European recipes?

We are the only restaurant that offers Maori kai in the Far North. Since we are successful in offering local cuisine, some tourist companies have included in their travel packages a visit to our restaurant.

We invite you to join us for a meal and enjoy an experience dining with us and others at the Haukai Restaurant. Our website offers many features to help you see why we are a restaurant that offers more than just a regular restaurant meal. Our application includes the following features:

  • A reservation form to reserve a table or tables at our restaurant.
  • A Google map with directions for you to find us.
  • A contact form to submit any enquiries you might have about our cuisine, location or the restaurant.
  • A photo gallery in which you can upload a photo of your time here at the restaurant.
  • Prayers and songs that you can download to learn the karakia and songs that will be sung at the restaurant.
  • Special Events page to find out when you can be part of a Hangi.
  • A video of the restaurant owners to tell you more about their background and the successes of their previous restaurants and their hopes for The Haukai Restaurant.

We can't wait any longer! Make a reservation now and experience the local cuisine of Maori and New Zealand European recipes. Thank you for your time and have a great day!
